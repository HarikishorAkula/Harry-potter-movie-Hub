Character Profiles

Harry Potter

The Boy Who Lived, famous for his lightning-shaped scar and his role in defeating Voldemort.

  • Birthday: July 31
  • Patronus: Stag
  • Favorite Spell: Expelliarmus

Hermione Granger

A brilliant witch with a love for books and learning, and one of Harry's best friends.

  • Birthday: September 19
  • Patronus: Otter
  • Favorite Subject: Arithmancy

Ron Weasley

Harry's loyal friend, known for his bravery and sense of humor.

  • Birthday: March 1
  • Patronus: Jack Russell Terrier
  • Favorite Quidditch Team: Chudley Cannons

Neville Longbottom

A brave and loyal friend, known for his growth from a timid boy to a courageous hero.

  • Birthday: July 30
  • Patronus: Non-corporeal
  • Favorite Subject: Herbology

Ginny Weasley

A talented witch and Harry's eventual wife, known for her fierce independence and bravery.

  • Birthday: August 11
  • Patronus: Horse
  • Favorite Spell: Bat-Bogey Hex
